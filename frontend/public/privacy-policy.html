Privacy Policy for BranchSense

Last updated: July 21, 2026

BranchSense ("we," "our," or "us") operates the BranchSense mobile application (Android) and web service, which helps students determine suitable engineering branches based on their academic details.

1. Information We Collect

BranchSense collects only the following information, provided directly by you:

  • Your name
  • Your board of study
  • Your passing year

We do not collect email addresses, phone numbers, location data, device identifiers, contacts, photos, or any other personal information beyond what is listed above. We do not require account creation or login.

2. How We Use Your Information

The information you provide is used solely to generate personalized branch/course recommendations and improve the accuracy of results shown to you. We do not sell, rent, or share this information with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Advertising

BranchSense may display advertisements served through Google AdMob. Google AdMob may use device identifiers and other technical data to serve ads, which may include personalized (interest-based) advertising.

Google's use of this data is governed by Google's own Privacy Policy: policies.google.com/privacy.

You can review and adjust your ad personalization settings at: adssettings.google.com.

Because BranchSense may be used by minors, users under the age of 13 are only shown non-personalized ads, in accordance with applicable child privacy laws (including COPPA).

4. Data Storage and Security

The limited information you provide (name, board of study, passing year) is stored securely and used only for the purpose of generating your recommendation results. We do not use any third-party analytics, crash-reporting, or tracking SDKs in the app or web service.

5. Children's Privacy

BranchSense's web service may be used by minors. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children beyond what is described in Section 1, and we do not use that information for purposes other than generating academic recommendations. Parents or guardians who believe their child has provided information beyond what is described here may contact us using the details below to request its removal.

6. Your Rights

You may contact us at any time to ask what information we hold about you, or to request correction or deletion of your information.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
