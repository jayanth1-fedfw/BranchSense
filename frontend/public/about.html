About BranchSense

BranchSense helps students figure out which engineering branch fits them best. Based on your board of study and passing year, BranchSense gives you clear, relevant guidance to help you make one of the most important decisions of your academic journey — choosing the right engineering branch.

Our goal is simple: take the guesswork out of branch selection and give students a straightforward starting point for their engineering future.

BranchSense is available on Android and as a web service.
